Privacy Policy

SonicDroplet keeps recordings on your Mac.

Effective date: March 12, 2026. SonicDroplet is designed to work locally and does not include cloud sync, analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.

1. Data Collection

SonicDroplet does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

2. Audio Recordings

Recordings are created locally on your Mac and saved only to locations you choose. SonicDroplet does not upload, share, or transmit recordings to external servers.

3. Permissions

SonicDroplet may request macOS audio capture permission so it can record the sound currently playing on your Mac using Apple's public Core Audio APIs. SonicDroplet does not record screen video.

4. Network Access

SonicDroplet is intended to function fully offline. It does not require an account or internet access to record, export, or play back your files.

5. Analytics and Tracking

SonicDroplet does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, telemetry, crash reporting services, or tracking pixels.

6. Third-Party Services

SonicDroplet does not integrate with third-party processing services for audio, storage, or identity.

7. Data Retention

SonicDroplet does not retain data outside the files you explicitly save on your device. You control when those files are kept or deleted.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.

9. Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the support page or open an issue on GitHub Issues.
